Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most frequently

asked questions on Start.NG

Is there any basic knowledge I should prepare for that will help me starting or does this course start at square 1?

You do not need to have any prior knowledge of software development to enroll in our program.

Does this course allow you to participate while working full-time?

Yes. There is a lot to learn, and it will be challenging. But our program is designed to fit into your schedule. You will need to dedicate at least 15 hours per week to be successful. But you'll be able to choose your own hours.

What happens if I quit midway?

We understand that software development can be challenging for a lot of people, especially beginners. We will do all we can to support you throughout the program. If you do decide to leave, you can re-enroll in the next session.

What are the chances of getting a job after this program?

We will be giving you all you need in terms of technical details to get a junior role, in addition we will be touching important concepts that'll generally help you land a job. However, getting a job still largely depends on the individual.

I’m not a very fast learner, do you think I can become a technology expert in 7 months?

Whatever your level of knowledge is about the industry, our instructors and mentors will meet you at that level and work with you to address your questions. While it is not possible to become an expert in just seven months (expertise takes years of experience), we will give you everything you need to get started and the resources required to grow.

What do I need to be a part of the program?

You need a computer with internet access, a passion to learn, determination to stick with it, and willingness to work with others

How many hours do I have to dedicate daily

At least 15 hours per week

When is the program starting?

The program starts on March 15, 2021, and enrolment closes by March 8.

Can I hire from training graduates?

The training is not enough for anyone to be good at the profession. You can hire them to intern with you, which means you are willing to help groom them

I filled the form but didn't receive any confirmation email, is that normal or should I apply again?

As long as you got the success message, you don't need to register again. However, you can attempt to register again using the same details, you should get a notification if your details already exist

Can I enroll even I am not from Nigeria?

Yes, you can. The training is a remote training and we welcome everyone from anywhere.

IPlease what the technical requirement of getting into this?

None, the training is for complete beginners
